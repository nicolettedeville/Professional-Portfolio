I make web stuff.

I make everything from websites, to webapps, to webanything! If it's on the web I can make it!

I'm Nicolette and I love to program!

7 years ago I started wondering what I should do with my life as a profession. Throughout the years I had tried many jobs and just couldn't find one that I wanted to settle on. My husband suggested programming and creating websites and now 7 years later, I just can't get enough. Websites, webapps, webscrapers, they just all are so much fun to build! So naturally I have made a few different websites in my time and devoted to so many aspects of life. From massage to vaping and so many in between I have built so much and I can't wait to build more.
